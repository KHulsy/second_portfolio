Prior to teaching, Kaitlin completed a Bachelor's of Arts in English, with a specialization in Literature in the Culture of Information from the University of California, Santa Barbara. She also has an M.F.A. in Creative Writing and Writing for the Performing Arts from the University of California, Riverside.She also holds a single-subject English credential from the University of LaVerne. Completion of her MFA required successful submission of a 120-page original manuscript. In March, Kaitlin also formed Operation Dulce. The organization has been instrumental in delivering care packages to medical professionals on the front lines. Kaitlin was an opener for the first all-female comedy showcase in Bakersfield, California. Her one-acts and sketches have been performed on-stage at the only donation-based theater in the country, The Empty Space Theater. Kaitlin is grateful to have learned multiple beautiful coding languages in a very small period of time.

She hopes to one day use her new-found powers for good.
